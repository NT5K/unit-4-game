Gem Game

Instructions:
You will be given a random number at the start of a new game.
Clicking a gem will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win by matching the total score with the random number.
You loose when the total score becomes greater than the random number.
When a new game starts, each gem will have a new, hidden random value.
